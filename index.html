[snake]: ~$


Click on the start button to begin a new game. A decimal number will appear at the top of your screen.


Your goal is to convert this number to binary by eating food bits in the correct order.


For example, if the number is 20. Your snake should eat a 1 then a 0.


Navigate the snake to the correct food bits using the arrow keys.


If you eat the correct food bit, your snake will grow. If you eat the incorrect food bit, your snake will lose segments.


Once a number has been converted, a new decimal number will be provided.


Game ends if your snake hits a wall or loses all of his segments
